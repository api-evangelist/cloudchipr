Cloudchipr argues that mature cloud cost visibility practices break down once AI enters the stack, since AI spend differs structurally in unit of cost, attribution, non-linear cost behavior, ownership, and tooling maturity. The post walks through five specific gaps between cloud and AI cost observability and argues organizations that invest in AI cost visibility now will gain an edge as AI becomes a major budget line.
